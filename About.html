About Of Luee

Luee Beauty

Making Every Face Perfect

Luee Beauty was founded in 2019 with a vision to bring high-quality skincare solutions that are friendly to all skin types. Starting from a commitment to combining natural ingredients with innovative technology, the company continues to grow into one of the most trusted brands in the beauty industry, helping customers achieve healthy and glowing skin.
about Luee is an award-winning hypoallergenic & natural skincare brand from Indonesia. Our main ingredients, niacinemaid and salicylic acid.

Our products are special because all our products contain niacinemide and salicylic acid which is high quality. All products are ultra-lightweight, soothing, extra-light, and non-comedogenic to provide a sweat-wicking, non-sticky,silky finish.
